  • 1. HOW FAST THE CASH BACK MONEY APPEAR IN MY PROFILE?

    Your earned cash back will appear in your profile within 5 hours from the time you had done the purchase in online shop. It will have status “pending”.?

  • 2. WHAT DOES THE STATUSES NEXT TO MY CASH BACK MEAN?

    Status “Pending” - your cash back is registered but money haven’t arrived yet - it could take up to couple of months depending on retailer return policy. You cannot redeem it yet.

    Status “OK” - your money is ready to be redeemed

    Status “Rejected” - the cash back have been rejected. Reason could be returned to the shop purchase.

    Status “Paid” - you have been redeeming this amount

  • 3. WHEN I CAN REDEEM MY MONEY?

    You can redeem your cash back when amount is not less than 1 Euro and it has status “OK”. Transfer to the bank account could take up to 1 week.
